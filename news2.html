Multiple M.Sc. and Ph.D. openings


Thursday, Jan 16, 2020


We have multiple M.Sc. and Ph.D. openings in the SAIN Lab. The candidates will be enrolled in the Computer Science program at Ontario Tech University, Canada. Successful applicants will be advised by one or two of the lab faculty members in any of the research focal areas of the lab. The openings come with research and teaching assistantship funding for the typical duration of the program


Qualifications

In addition to minimum requirements for Ph.D. or M.Sc. admission, candidates are expected to have a degree in computer science, computer engineering, mathematics, statistics, or any other related field. Candidates must have also a strong background in statistics and programming. Prior knowledge of machine learning is also an asset. Candidates should be enthusiastic about learning new methods and applying their expertise in innovative ways. Prior experience in interdisciplinary research will be an asset.

Application

The positions start from either September 1st, 2020 or January 1st 2021 (based on mutual agreement). Before applying formally for a position through Ontario Tech graduate studies, the interested applicants should submit the cover letter along with CV, transcripts, and a sample of publication (if any) by email to abari@uoit.ca and julie.thorpe@uoit.ca.
